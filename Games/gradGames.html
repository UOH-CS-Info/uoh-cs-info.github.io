About this dashboard

The University of Hull Graduate Games Dashboard lists over 560 commercially released video games that carry credits from University of Hull Games Programming graduates, spanning from the early 1990s to 2025. Graduates have collectively shipped titles that sold more than one billion copies worldwide and won over 100 industry awards including BAFTAs, DICE Awards, and Golden Joysticks. Top-rated titles include Grand Theft Auto IV (Metacritic 98), Grand Theft Auto V (97), Red Dead Redemption II (97), The Orange Box (96), LittleBigPlanet (95), Red Dead Redemption (95), God of War (94), Forza Horizon 4 and 5 (92 each), Battlefield 2 (91), and Empire: Total War (90). The dashboard is interactive: users can filter by release year, genre, and Metacritic score band, and sort the full games table by any column. Hull offers a BSc/MEng and MSc in Computer Science for Games Programming. Contact: hull.ac.uk · #HullGamesProg
